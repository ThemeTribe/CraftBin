Kindergarten Trophies and Medals

Affordable MDF Wood Trophies for Little Learners

Looking for a delightful and budget-friendly way to celebrate your kindergarteners? Our MDF Wooden Awards are specially designed to honour the efforts and achievements of young learners.

Crafted from smooth, kid-friendly MDF wood, these charming awards offer a wonderful keepsake to recognise their milestones. (Average height is approximately 170mm).

To make recognition easy and affordable, basic engraving is included free of charge. We can add the award category, child's name, and date where applicable.

  • Perfect for Little Learners: Kid-friendly MDF wood design.
  • Budget-Friendly Choice: Celebrate achievements affordably.
  • Free Basic Engraving Included: Personalise at no extra cost.
  • Ideal Size for Kids: Approx. 170mm tall.
  • Charming Keepsake: A lovely memento of their progress.

CraftBin creates custom laser-cut acrylic trophies, keyrings, and MDF boxes for every occasion. Based in the Vaal Triangle, we offer creative solutions for commemorative, academic, and activity awards.
