Tall Acrylic Trophies

Celebrate the milestone

Give your award ceremony a touch of class and distinction with our Tall Custom Acrylic Trophies.

Designed to honour the top three achievers in style, these trophies come in various heights and are perfect for celebrating 1st, 2nd, and 3rd place winners.

These trophies are specifically designed according the the occasion or event. Individual trophies are available if a individual trophy of a specific height is required.

Specifications

The basic design, where the engraving is done directly onto the clear acrylic, may be enhanced by having the engraving done on a silver or gold metallic plate which is attached to bottom part of the clear acrylic Or having a metallic plate fitted to the base of the trophy.

Option 1 consists of the engraved metallic plate and a black acrylic backing attached to the back of the trophy to enhance the appearance of the trophy. Option 2 is fitted directly to the base of the trophy.

Approx measurements based on the above example.
