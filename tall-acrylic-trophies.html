Tall Acrylic Trophies

Honour Achievements with Tall Custom Trophies

Add a touch of class and distinction to your award ceremony with our Tall Custom Acrylic Trophies. Designed to stand out, these trophies are perfect for celebrating significant milestones and honouring top achievers.

Available in various heights, they are ideal for recognizing 1st, 2nd, and 3rd place winners in style. We design these trophies specifically for your occasion or event. Individual custom trophies of a specific height are also available upon request.

Customization & Specification Options

Tailor your tall acrylic trophy to perfectly match your event. While the basic design features engraving directly onto the clear acrylic, you have options to enhance its appearance with metallic plates:

  • Option 1: Engraved Metallic Plate with Backing: Your engraving is applied to a silver or gold metallic plate. This plate is then attached to the lower section of the clear acrylic award, paired with a black acrylic backing to make the engraving stand out and enhance the trophy's overall look.
  • Option 2: Metallic Base Plate: A silver or gold metallic plate is fitted directly onto the base of the trophy, providing a classic location for your custom engraving.
