Laser-Cut, Trophies, Key Rings And Boxes

Craftbin-Vaal specialises in acrylic trophies, key rings, utility boxes, and gift boxes. Based in Meyerton in the Vaal Triangle, we are a small business dedicated to providing personalised service and ensuring customer satisfaction.

Laser-cut Acrylic Trophies

Laser-cut acrylic trophies are a sleek and modern way to recognize achievement or commemorate special events. Acrylic is a versatile material that can be easily cut, engraved, and customized to create stunning, professional-looking trophies. Our trophies have our unique, black acrylic base, which adds to appearance of the trophy. The design of the trophy can range from minimalistic to complex, depending on the requirements. Shapes can range from geometric patterns to more elaborate forms to represent the event or occasion. We have a range of standard designs to choose from, as well as custom designs in accordance with customer’s preferences
