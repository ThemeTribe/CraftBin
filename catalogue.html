Benefits Of Acrylic Trophies

  • Acrylic laser-cut trophies have a sleek, modern look that is often preferred for contemporary awards, adding a touch of sophistication to the presentation
  • Acrylic has excellent engraving properties, producing very clear , crisp and defined engravings.
  • Acrylic trophies can be produced in a variety of shapes, sizes, allowing for a wide range of design possibilities to match the theme or event.
  • Acrylic is much lighter than glass or metal, which makes the trophies easier to handle, transport, and display without compromising on aesthetic appeal.
  • Acrylic is strong and resistant to shattering compared to glass, making it a more durable and safer option for trophies. It also resists yellowing or fading over time
  • Acrylic is generally more affordable than other materials like glass or crystal, while still providing a premium look and feel.
